Flavour Locks is part of

founded in 2016

Flavour Locks is part of Foodlands Group Company, founded in 2016, The group was formed to deliver natural and healthy food stuffs at the doorsteps in a professional way. Flavour locks strictly follows and makes sure that all products are made out of premium quality ingredients, manufactured at our own hygienic factory with the help of next generation processing methodology and packing machines.

Our vast knowledge of the domains has made us immensely popular in South India. We work with an aim to offer maximum client satisfaction with our range of products along with attractive & innovative packaging and on time delivery. This has earned us a large base of loyal customers.

WHAT WE DO

Love for natural and mother’s
homemade food.

We a group of youngsters set out on a journey with a big dream of making natural and homemade food items which cater to the need of people who are away from their home and crave for mother's homemade food . With fresh resolve to make healthy and purely natural authentic traditional homemade food, we started off with a small kitchen in the Electronic City of Bangalore

WHAT WE DO

We at food lands believe that if there is a recipe for success in life it starts with picking the right ingredients. We have been following this through in making each and every product of ours.The ingredients that we use in our idli/dosa batter are the best quality rice right from the farm to our factory /the finest urad dal and RO purified water. We care for your health. They provide a balanced contribution of carbohydrate ,proteins and helps in preparing a quick and healthy meal. The secret ingredient that goes into our batter is love for good food/ trust and accountability that you share with us.From our factory to your door steps we travel the path of trust/love/goodwill and reliability .It is a path that helps you to make your cooking hassle free. Our products are prepared in such a manner that it becomes very convenient and easy to make soft and fluffy idles and crispy dosas.

Our brand has become a household name only because of the trust and accountability that people have on us. Our idli dosa batter from flavour locks are the best. They help to make soft / fluffy idlies and crispy dosas. We assure you that it will definitely remind you of your mother’s home made idli and dosa which you have always yearned for when you are away from home.In this world of hustle and bustle everybody is on their heels. Everyone wants to be healthy but doesn’t have time to follow the traditional method of making their favourite breakfast like idli and dosa which are both healthy and nutritious and less in calories.

Resolution

Our resolution is to make healthy ,purely natural authentic ,traditional homemade food which will cater to the need of people who are away from their home and crave for mother’s home made food.

Aim

To cater to the need of all the people who neither have time to depend on restaurants everyday,nor have time to prepare food in the good old traditional method. This is where our flavour lock products come to your rescue, that too straight at your door steps.

Principles

Our principle in life is to follow the proverb "health is wealth". Health is the greatest of human blessings and food is the best medicine to stay healthy. Hence we religiously follow the proverb while preparing our products which is always mixed with a pinch of love and trust.
